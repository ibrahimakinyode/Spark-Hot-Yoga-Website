Spark Hot Yoga — Website Design
A website design concept for Spark Hot Yoga, a hot yoga and wellness studio based in Snohomish, WA.
Overview
The design centers on a warm, editorial aesthetic that balances energy with calm — reflecting the studio's dual focus on powerful, sweat-driven classes and slow, restorative practice. The goal was to create a site that feels welcoming to new students while giving existing members quick access to booking and perks.
Key Sections
Hero — Bold tagline ("There is a Spark in you. Let it shine.") paired with a warm, candid studio photo to set an inviting, human tone from the first scroll.
Class Showcase — A carousel highlighting signature classes (Power/Yin, Slow Flow) with direct "Book a Class" CTAs for each.
Social Proof — A full-width testimonial section using a real student quote to build trust.
Community & Perks — Three-column layout guiding visitors toward next steps: getting started, becoming a member, or diving deeper into workshops and retreats.
Footer — Quick links, FAQ/contact resources, and app download badges for convenience.
Design Goals
Approachable, non-intimidating tone for first-time visitors
Clear, repeated calls to action (booking, membership, app download)
Earthy, textured color palette (cream, sage, deep teal) to feel grounded rather than clinical
Mobile-first layout, since most traffic for boutique studios comes from phones
